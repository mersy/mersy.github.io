.htaccessを作成して、mt-staticにおく

<IfModule mod_rewite.c>
RewriteEngine on
RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-d
RewriteCond %{REQUEST_FILENAME} !-f
RewriteRule ^mt-static/(.*)$ /var/www/html/mt/plugins/mt-static/$1 [L,QSA]
</IfModule>
